Alongside learning to code using HTML5, JavaScript, CSS and Python, James is currently carrying out research towards an MPhil in EuroLit at Cambridge University, having already graduated with a First in Comparative Literature and French from University College London.

In his spare time, James leverages his passion for nature and wildlife by volunteering as an amphibian surveyor in the Cambridge area, ensuring the survival of local species.

Passionate about politics, James works with local groups to encourage students to vote. He also aspires to a career in politics and is currently developing his political style through his role as Postgraduate School Representative on the Council of the School of Arts & Humanities, Cambridge SU.
